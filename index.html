about me

I am JS developer who ...
Completed some of the best premium Udemy courses, read several books about web development, including Eloquent Javascript, You Don't Know JS... Coded a dozen websites on my own, besides others that I coded-along as practice for the skills taught in the courses.

I love clean, simple, and fun design that is easy to navigate, responsive, and intuitive to use. Wrote a practice apps to learn to program in pure Javascript. I work primarily with HTML, CSS/SCSS, & JavaScript and venturing on to learn React, and my next plan is to raise my knowledge to a higher level in Next.js.

Have experience in designing, developing, deploying, and maintaining websites while communicating with clients.
I love that learning programming led me into a world that is analytical and creative, with something new to learn every day. I am on the look-out for remote and contract work as a Front-end-developer.
