about me

Hi, I`m Bojan, a self-taught frontend developer.

For over a year I learn how to create amazing things on the web.
In the beginning, I had zero knowledge of that how to start programming. Luckily, I love to search and learn. I found some great courses and books like Eloquent Javascript and You Don't Know JS. That was my guide on how to become a front-end developer from scratch. It took me 12 months to complete the path. At the same time, I finished a freeCodeCamp curriculum for responsive web design and start to build my projects.

Now I have experience coding websites and applications using React framework . I'm planning to raise my knowledge to a higher level in Next.js, and at the same time, I search for new technologies and trends.

Being self-taught is hard. When I started, the scariest thing was how to face problems I struggled with, and couldn’t move further because of it. But during the time I learned how to solve it, sometimes it took me days and numerous attempts until I find the right solution. My passion is web development. It gives me the possibility to challenge myself and use a mix of technologies and creativity. I am always experimenting, always learning, and never bored. Here are the technologies and tools I use:
